Dlo

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 19, 2026

Overview

Dlo is a local file manager and browser for saving, organizing, previewing, and managing files on your device. Dlo is designed to stay simple, private, and focused on files that users are allowed to save and manage.

The app does not require an account for its core features. Files saved in Dlo remain on the user's device unless the user chooses to share, export, delete, or move them.

Data Collection

Dlo does not collect personal data from the app for tracking, advertising, or resale.

The app may store local browsing data such as bookmarks, history, tabs, file records, folder choices, and download records on the device so the app can provide its features. This information is stored locally and is not sent to Dlo.

Purchases

Subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed by Apple. Dlo does not receive full payment card details.

Dlo may use RevenueCat to manage subscription status, validate purchases, and restore purchases. RevenueCat may process purchase information associated with an anonymous app user identifier so the app can unlock Pro features and keep subscription access in sync.

Diagnostics

Apple may provide developers with crash logs, diagnostics, and aggregated performance information depending on the user's device settings.

Third-Party Websites

Dlo includes a browser. Websites visited by the user are operated by third parties and are governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

Dlo does not control third-party websites, their content, or their download permissions. Some websites may restrict downloading, saving, offline access, or reuse of their content.

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring that they have the right to save, store, view, or share any file, and for complying with applicable laws, copyright rules, and each third-party website's terms.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact support@downlo.app.
