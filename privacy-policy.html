Downlo

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

Overview

Downlo is a private file manager and browser for saving, organizing, and playing files on your iPhone. The app does not require an account for its core features, and files saved in Downlo remain on the user's device unless the user chooses to share, export, delete, or move them.

Data Collection

Downlo does not collect personal data from the app for tracking, advertising, or resale.

The app may store local browsing data such as bookmarks, history, tabs, file records, folder choices, and download records on the device so the app can provide its features. This information is stored locally and is not sent to Downlo.

Purchases

If subscriptions or in-app purchases are enabled, purchases are processed by Apple. Downlo does not receive full payment card details.

Diagnostics

Apple may provide developers with crash logs, diagnostics, and aggregated performance information depending on the user's device settings.

Third-Party Websites

Downlo includes a browser. Websites visited by the user are operated by third parties and are governed by their own privacy policies and terms. Users are responsible for following each website's terms and for saving files only when they have the right or permission to do so.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact support@downlo.app.
